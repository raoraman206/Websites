Our Story

Born from Passion,
Brewed with Purpose

✦

Ember & Ground began in a narrow cobblestone lane in 2017, founded by two friends who believed that exceptional coffee deserved an exceptional setting. We source single-origin beans from small family farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — relationships built on trust, fairness, and a shared reverence for the craft.

Every detail of our space — the worn leather chairs, the low amber lighting, the hand-lettered chalkboards — is an invitation to slow down. To be present. To let the cup in your hands become a small, perfect moment.

We don't just serve coffee. We tend to the quiet spaces between conversations.

— Ember & Ground
